<p><p>Three-phase smart meters are digital devices that measure electrical energy consumption in industrial, commercial, and residential settings. Direct current meters measure energy consumption in a straight line, giving accurate readings for systems that use DC power sources. Alternating current meters, on the other hand, measure energy consumption for systems using AC power sources. Both types of meters provide real-time data on energy usage, allowing users to monitor and manage their electricity consumption effectively.</p></p>
