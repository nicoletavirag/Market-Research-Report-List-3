Additionally, the growing geriatric population and changing lifestyles contributing to the increasing incidence of rheumatoid arthritis are also driving the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Disease-Modifying Antirheumatic Drug Market is the increasing adoption of biologic DMARDs, which are derived from living organisms and are more targeted and effective compared to traditional synthetic DMARDs. Biologics offer improved disease control, reduced risk of side effects, and better quality of life for patients with rheumatoid arthritis. As a result, pharmaceutical companies are investing in research and development of new biologic DMARDs, further driving market growth in this segment.</p></p>
