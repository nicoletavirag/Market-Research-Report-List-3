<p><ul><li>Probe and Drogue Type</li><li>Boom and Receptacle Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Aerial Refueling (AAR) Systems utilize two main types of systems: Probe and Drogue Type, and Boom and Receptacle Type. The Probe and Drogue Type uses a flexible hose and a probe on the receiving aircraft to make the connection, while the Boom and Receptacle Type involves a rigid telescoping tube extending from the tanker aircraft to connect with a receptacle on the receiver. Both systems provide efficient and precise refueling capabilities, allowing for increased mission duration and operational flexibility for military aircraft.</p></p>
