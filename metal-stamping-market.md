<p><ul><li>Blanking Process</li><li>Embossing Process</li><li>Bending Process</li><li>Coining Process</li><li>Flanging Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal stamping market involves various processes including blanking, embossing, bending, coining, and flanging. Blanking process involves cutting out a shape from a metal sheet. Embossing process creates raised designs on the surface of the metal. Bending process is used to bend the metal into different shapes. Coining process is used to create detailed designs on the metal surface. Flanging process involves creating a flange or lip on the edge of the metal sheet. These processes cater to different manufacturing needs in various industries.</p></p>
