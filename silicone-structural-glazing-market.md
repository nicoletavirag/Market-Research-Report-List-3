<p><p>Silicone structural glazing market types include four-sided structural, two-sided structural, slope, stepped glass, U-shaped, total vision systems, and others. Four-sided structural glazing involves bonding glass to the entire perimeter of the frame, while two-sided structural glazing bonds glass to two sides. Slope glazing is installed at an angle, while stepped glass features multiple levels. U-shaped systems create a channel for glass insertion, and total vision systems offer seamless views. The aluminum structural framing market provides the framework for these various types of structural glazing installations.</p></p>
