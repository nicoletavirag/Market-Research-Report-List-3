<p><strong>The Overhead Travelling Cranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Top Running Overhead Cranes</li><li>Under-Slung Overhead Cranes</li><li>Freestanding Gantry Systems</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Overhead travelling cranes are segmented into different types based on their structure and design. Conventional top running overhead cranes are the most common type, with the crane running on a track at the top of the building. Under-slung overhead cranes run on tracks mounted on the bottom of the building's support structure. Freestanding gantry systems are standalone structures with wheels that can move the crane horizontally. Other types of overhead travelling cranes may include wall-mounted cranes, stacker cranes, and jib cranes. Each type offers specific benefits and applications based on the needs of the user.</p></p>
