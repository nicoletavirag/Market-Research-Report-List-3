<p><p>Conventional Island Deaerators are used in power plants to remove dissolved gases from feedwater to prevent corrosion and improve efficiency. The three common types of deaerators are Tray Type Deaerators, which use trays to distribute water over a large surface area for efficient deaeration, Spray Deaerators, which use high-pressure sprays to break up water into small droplets for effective gas removal, and Membrane Deaerators, which use a hydrophobic membrane to remove gases through a vacuum process. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications.</p></p>
