Buoys that are less than 1 meter are often used in small bodies of water such as lakes and rivers. The 1-3 meter buoys are commonly found in coastal areas and larger waterways. Buoys that are more than 3 meters in size are typically used in deep-sea applications and offshore locations where increased visibility and stability are required.</p></p>
