<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metalcasting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Casting</li><li>High-Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Low-Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metalcasting market can be divided into different types based on the casting process used. Gravity casting relies on gravity to fill the mold with molten metal. High-pressure die casting involves forcing molten metal into a mold using high pressure. Low-pressure die casting uses lower pressure to achieve more intricate designs. Sand casting is a process where a mold is created by compacting sand around a pattern, and then molten metal is poured into the mold. Each type of casting process has its own advantages and applications in the metalcasting market.</p></p>
