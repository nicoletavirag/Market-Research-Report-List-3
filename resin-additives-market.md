Plasticizers enhance flexibility and durability. Antioxidants protect against degradation, stabilizers prevent deterioration from heat or UV exposure, and other additives provide various benefits like flame retardancy or improved adhesion. Each type of additive serves a specific purpose in enhancing the performance and characteristics of resins for different applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and packaging.</p></p>
