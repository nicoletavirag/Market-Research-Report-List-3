<p><ul><li>Laser Mask Writer</li><li>Electron Beam Mask Writer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mask writers are essential tools used in semiconductor manufacturing to create patterns on masks, which are then transferred onto silicon wafers to create integrated circuits. Two main types of mask writers in the market are the laser mask writer and electron beam mask writer. Laser mask writers use laser beams to write patterns on masks, while electron beam mask writers use highly focused electron beams for the same purpose. Both technologies are critical for the production of advanced semiconductor devices with increasingly smaller features.</p></p>
