<p><p>Pellet dosing apparatus market includes three main types: weigh, volumetric, and count dosing systems. Weigh dosing systems measure the precise weight of each pellet being dispensed, ensuring accuracy in dosage. Volumetric dosing systems measure the volume of pellets being dispensed, providing a cost-effective option for handling large quantities. Count dosing systems dispense a set number of pellets per dose, ideal for applications requiring uniformity in dosages. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
