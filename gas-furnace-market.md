<p><ul><li>Single-stage Gas Furnaces</li><li>Two-stage Gas Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas furnace market includes single-stage gas furnaces and two-stage gas furnaces. Single-stage gas furnaces operate at full capacity whenever they are turned on, providing consistent heating output. Two-stage gas furnaces have two levels of heating output, allowing them to operate at a lower capacity for more efficient heating when full capacity is not needed. Two-stage gas furnaces are typically more energy-efficient and provide more even heating throughout the home compared to single-stage gas furnaces.</p></p>
