The 3dB waveguide combiner/divider evenly splits an input signal into two output signals with a 3dB loss. The 3dB hybrid waveguide combiner/divider also splits signals but with the added ability to combine signals as well. Variable power divider allows for adjusting the power splitting ratio, providing flexibility in signal distribution.
