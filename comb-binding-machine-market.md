<p><p>Comb binding machines come in two main types: manual binding machines and electric binding machines. Manual binding machines require the user to physically turn a handle to open and close the comb, making them more affordable and suitable for occasional use. On the other hand, electric binding machines automate the comb binding process, making them faster and easier to use, but also more expensive. Both types of machines are commonly used for binding reports, presentations, and other documents in offices and businesses.</p></p>
