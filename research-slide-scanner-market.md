<p><p>Research slide scanners are widely used in laboratories for digitalizing and analyzing microscope slides. There are two main types of market for slide scanners: Brightfield and Fluorescence. Brightfield scanners are commonly used for routine pathology and cytology applications, as they provide high resolution images of stained tissues. On the other hand, Fluorescence scanners are ideal for imaging samples that emit fluorescent signals, making them suitable for studies involving proteins, DNA, and other biological molecules. Both types cater to the diverse needs of researchers and clinicians in the healthcare and life science industries.</p></p>
