<p><p>Tin-plated metal cans for food come in two main market types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are made by welding together three separate pieces: the body, bottom, and lid. These cans are commonly used for packaging fruits, vegetables, and soups. Two-piece cans, on the other hand, are made by forming the body and bottom from a single piece of metal, with the lid being sealed on afterwards. Two-piece cans are often used for packaging beverages and canned meats.</p></p>
