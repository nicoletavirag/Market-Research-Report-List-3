<p><strong>The 3D Imaging TOF Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ToF (dToF)</li><li>Indirect ToF (iToF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D imaging TOF sensor market includes Direct ToF (dToF) and Indirect ToF (iToF) types. dToF sensors measure the distance by calculating the time taken for light to travel to the object and back. iToF sensors estimate the distance indirectly through techniques like triangulation or structured light. Both types are used in various applications such as smartphones, autonomous vehicles, robotics, and healthcare. Each type offers unique advantages and limitations, catering to different industry requirements.</p></p>
