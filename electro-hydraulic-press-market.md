<p><p>Electro-Hydraulic Press market includes two main types- Horizontal and Vertical presses. Horizontal Electro-Hydraulic Presses are designed for applications requiring a horizontal pressing force, such as bending, straightening, or punching. They are ideal for larger workpieces that need to be supported evenly across the pressing area. Vertical Electro-Hydraulic Presses, on the other hand, are suitable for applications that require a vertical pressing force, such as molding, forming, or assembling. They are commonly used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
