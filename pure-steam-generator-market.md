<p><p>Pure Steam Generator market consists of three main types: Re-boiler Generator, Thermosyphon Generator/ External Rising Film Evaporator, and Falling Film Evaporator. Re-boiler Generator operates by utilizing the waste heat from an external source to generate pure steam. Thermosyphon Generator relies on natural circulation to generate steam, while Falling Film Evaporator utilizes a falling film of water to generate steam. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in various industries, such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and power generation.</p></p>
