<p><p>Micro Electro Mechanical Systems (MEMS) are miniaturized mechanical and electromechanical devices that are integrated onto semiconductor chips. The MEMS market can be categorized into four main types: Sensing MEMS, which includes accelerometers and gyroscopes for sensing motion and orientation; Bio MEMS, used in biomedical applications such as lab-on-a-chip devices; Optical MEMS, including mirror arrays for projection displays; and Radio Frequency (RF) MEMS, used in wireless communication applications such as tunable capacitors and switches. Each type of MEMS market caters to different industries and applications.</p></p>
