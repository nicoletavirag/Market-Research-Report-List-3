<p><ul><li>DC Microgrid</li><li>AC Microgrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Independent microgrid market types include DC microgrids, which operate on direct current, and AC microgrids, which operate on alternating current. DC microgrids are often simpler, with fewer components and higher efficiency, making them suitable for small-scale applications. AC microgrids, on the other hand, are more commonly used in larger systems and integrate more easily with the existing power grid infrastructure. Both types offer benefits in terms of resilience, flexibility, and control over energy generation and consumption.</p></p>
