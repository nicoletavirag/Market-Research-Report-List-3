<p><p>Unsaturated Polyester Resin (UPR) is widely used in composites due to its excellent properties such as corrosion resistance, low cost, and ease of processing. The three main types of UPR in the market are Orthopthalic, Isopthalic, and Dicyclopentadiene (DCPD). Orthopthalic UPR is commonly used for general purpose applications, Isopthalic UPR offers higher chemical and heat resistance, and DCPD UPR provides improved impact resistance and toughness. Each type caters to specific industry needs, making UPR a versatile material for various applications.</p></p>
