<p><strong>The Deionized Water System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-bed Deionized Water System</li><li>Mixed-bed Deionized Water System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deionized water systems are used to remove minerals and impurities from water, making it suitable for use in various industries. Two-bed deionized water systems use separate tanks containing cation and anion exchange resins to remove specific ions from water. Mixed-bed deionized water systems mix cation and anion exchange resins together in a single tank, resulting in higher purity water. Both types of deionized water systems are popular in industries such as pharmaceuticals, electronics, and power generation.</p></p>
