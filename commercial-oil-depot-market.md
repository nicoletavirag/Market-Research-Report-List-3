<p><p>Independent oil depots are privately owned facilities that store and distribute petroleum products independently of any major oil company. They typically cater to smaller local markets and may offer a variety of services such as bulk storage, blending, and distribution. On the other hand, enterprise affiliated oil depots are facilities that are owned or operated by major oil companies. They are typically larger in scale and serve broader regional or national markets, often exclusively handling products from their affiliated company.</p></p>
