<p><p>Rigid box corner pasting machines are available in two main market types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention to feed the boxes and position them correctly for corner pasting. Fully automatic machines, on the other hand, are completely automated and do not require any manual intervention once the boxes are loaded. Both types of machines are popular in the packaging industry, with semi-automatic machines being more cost-effective for smaller production runs and fully automatic machines ideal for high-volume production.</p></p>
