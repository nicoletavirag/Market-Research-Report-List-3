<p><p>Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Devices (OVDs) are used in eye surgeries to protect the cornea, maintain space in the eye, and improve visualization. There are four main types of OVDs in the market: Dispersive OVDs, which spread out evenly on the cornea; Cohesive OVDs, which stay together as a cohesive gel; Combination OVDs, which combine properties of both dispersive and cohesive OVDs; and Visco-Adaptive OVDs, which change their viscosity depending on the conditions in the eye during surgery.</p></p>
