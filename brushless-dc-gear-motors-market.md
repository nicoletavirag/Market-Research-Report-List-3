<p><p>Brushless DC gear motors are categorized into three types: Parallel Shaft Gear Motors, Right-Angle Shaft Gear Motors, and Planetary Gear Motors. Parallel Shaft Gear Motors are suitable for applications where space is limited, while Right-Angle Shaft Gear Motors are used in applications where a 90-degree turn is necessary. Planetary Gear Motors offer high torque and efficiency, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Each type of gear motor serves a specific purpose and is chosen based on the requirements of the application.</p></p>
