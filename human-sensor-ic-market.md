They are commonly used in security systems and automatic lighting. </p><p>Thermopile Sensors measure temperature differences using multiple thermocouples connected in series. They are used in applications like detecting body temperature or monitoring occupancy in buildings. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in improving the efficiency and functionality of human detection systems.</p></p>
