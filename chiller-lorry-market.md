<p><p>Chiller lorries can be divided into two main market types: Single Temperature Type and Multi-Temperature Type. Single Temperature Type chiller lorries maintain a consistent temperature throughout the vehicle, making them suitable for transporting goods that require a uniform temperature setting. On the other hand, Multi-Temperature Type chiller lorries have multiple compartments with different temperature settings, allowing for the simultaneous transport of goods requiring different temperature conditions. Both types cater to different logistical needs and requirements in the transportation of temperature-sensitive goods.</p></p>
