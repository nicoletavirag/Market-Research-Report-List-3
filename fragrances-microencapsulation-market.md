<p><ul><li>Physico-Mechanical Methods</li><li>Chemical Methods</li><li>Physico-Chemical Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fragrances microencapsulation market can be classified into three types based on the methods used: Physico-Mechanical Methods, Chemical Methods, and Physico-Chemical Methods. Physico-Mechanical Methods involve physical processes like spray drying or fluidized bed coating. Chemical Methods use chemical reactions to encapsulate fragrances, such as coacervation or interfacial polymerization. Physico-Chemical Methods combine both physical and chemical processes, like solvent evaporation or emulsion polymerization. Each method offers different advantages and applications in the fragrance industry.</p></p>
