The market is divided into three main types: Computed Radiography (CR), Digital Radiography (DR), and Computed Tomography (CT). CR uses a digital imaging plate to capture X-ray images, while DR uses digital detectors for immediate image results. CT creates detailed 3D images using cross-sectional X-ray scans. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of image quality, speed, and functionality to cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
