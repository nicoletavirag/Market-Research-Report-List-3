<p><p>Wireless medical alert systems use different types of technology for communication. Zigbee, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and Cellular are the most common types in the market. Zigbee is a low-power, short-range wireless communication protocol, ideal for small medical alert devices. Wi-Fi offers high-speed internet connectivity for sending alerts. Bluetooth is used for short-range communication between devices. Cellular technology allows for alerts to be sent using a cellular network. Other technologies may include satellite communication or proprietary protocols.</p></p>
