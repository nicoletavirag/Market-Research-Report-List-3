<p><p>Heavy truck axle market can be categorized into two main types – Drive Axle and Non-Drive Axle. Drive Axle is responsible for transferring power from the transmission to the wheels, providing the necessary torque for the truck to move. Non-Drive Axle, on the other hand, does not transmit power but supports the weight of the truck and helps with steering. Both types are essential components in heavy trucks, with each playing a specific role in the overall performance and functionality of the vehicle.</p></p>
