<p><strong>The Immune Anti-Inhibitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Recombinant Immune Anti-Inhibitor</li><li>Plasma-derived Immune Anti-Inhibitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Recombinant Immune Anti-Inhibitors are developed through genetic engineering techniques, using human DNA to produce proteins that can help control inhibitors in patients with hemophilia. On the other hand, Plasma-derived Immune Anti-Inhibitors are obtained from pooled human plasma and contain naturally occurring antibodies that can neutralize inhibitors in the blood. Both types of immune anti-inhibitors are used in the treatment of hemophilia patients to prevent and manage the development of inhibitors against clotting factors.</p></p>
