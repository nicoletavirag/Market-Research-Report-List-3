<p><p>Flame Arresters Market Types include Dry-Type Flame Arresters and Wet-Type Flame Arresters. Dry-Type Flame Arresters work by quenching flames through the use of a mesh or perforated plate, preventing their propagation. On the other hand, Wet-Type Flame Arresters use a liquid, such as water or oil, to extinguish flames and cool down the system. Both types of flame arresters are crucial in industries where there is a risk of explosion or fire, ensuring the safety of personnel and equipment.</p></p>
