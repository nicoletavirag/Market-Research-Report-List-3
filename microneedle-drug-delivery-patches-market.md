<p><p>Microneedle drug delivery patches come in different types, including dissolving and insoluble. Dissolving type patches are designed to deliver drugs through the dissolution of the microneedles in the skin, allowing for controlled release of medication. Insoluble type patches, on the other hand, utilize solid microneedles that penetrate the skin and stay intact, facilitating drug delivery through diffusion. Both types offer advantages in terms of convenience, painlessness, and precise dosing, making them promising alternatives to traditional drug delivery methods.</p></p>
