<p><p>Traditional palletizers are the conventional machines that stack products onto pallets in a predetermined pattern using mechanical arms or gantries. On the other hand, robotic palletizers use robotic arms and systems to automate the palletizing process, offering greater flexibility and efficiency. Both types of palletizers are widely used in various industries, with traditional palletizers being more cost-effective for simple applications, while robotic palletizers are preferred for their versatility and advanced capabilities for complex and high-speed operations.</p></p>
