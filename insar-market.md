<p><strong>The InSAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) Images</li><li>Multiple Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) Images</li></ul></p>
<p><p>InSAR Market Types refer to the different ways in which Interferometric Synthetic Aperture Radar (InSAR) technology is utilized. Two SAR images involve comparing pairs of SAR images to measure ground surface deformation. Multiple SAR images involve analyzing multiple sets of SAR images to detect even smaller ground surface changes over time. Both methods are used in various industries such as environmental monitoring, infrastructure management, and disaster risk reduction to track and predict ground movements with high precision and accuracy.</p></p>
