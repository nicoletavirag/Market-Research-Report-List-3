<p><strong>The Portable X-Ray Flat Panel Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Conversion</li><li>Indirect Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable X-Ray Flat Panel Detectors are available in two types: Direct Conversion and Indirect Conversion. Direct Conversion detectors use a material that converts x-rays directly into electrical signals, offering higher image quality and efficiency. On the other hand, Indirect Conversion detectors use a scintillator to convert x-rays into visible light, which is then converted into electrical signals. Although Indirect Conversion detectors are more commonly used due to lower cost, Direct Conversion detectors are gaining popularity for their superior image quality.</p></p>
