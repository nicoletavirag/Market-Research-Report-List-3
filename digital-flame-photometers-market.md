<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li><li>Multi Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital flame photometers are available in three market types: single channel, dual channel, and multi channel. Single channel flame photometers are equipped with one detection channel, making them suitable for basic analysis of one element at a time. Dual channel flame photometers have two detection channels, allowing for simultaneous analysis of two elements. Multi channel flame photometers are designed with more than two detection channels, enabling the analysis of multiple elements simultaneously. Each market type caters to a different level of analysis complexity and throughput.</p></p>
