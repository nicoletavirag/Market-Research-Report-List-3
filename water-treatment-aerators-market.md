<p><p>Water Treatment Aerators Market is classified into three types- Immersed Aerators, Surface Aerators, and Others. Immersed Aerators are submerged in water and release oxygen through a diffuser, effectively treating water by promoting microbial activity. Surface Aerators create turbulence at the water's surface to increase oxygen transfer and break down pollutants. Other types include diffused air systems and floating aerators that offer unique water treatment solutions. Each type of aerator plays a crucial role in improving water quality and enhancing the efficiency of water treatment processes.</p></p>
