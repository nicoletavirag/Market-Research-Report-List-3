<p><p>Percutaneous Transluminal Angioplasty (PTA) Balloons are used in minimally invasive procedures to treat blocked arteries. Drug Eluting Balloons release medication to prevent reblockage after treatment. Normal Balloons are used for standard angioplasty procedures. Scoring Balloons have small blades that help create controlled cuts in the artery wall to improve flexibility. Cutting Balloons have larger blades that can make deeper cuts to treat tougher blockages. Each type of PTA balloon serves a specific purpose in opening up blocked arteries and improving blood flow.</p></p>
