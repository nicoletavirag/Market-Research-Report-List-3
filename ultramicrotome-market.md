<p><p>Ultramicrotomes are divided into two main types: Tactile Control and Computer Control. Tactile Control ultramicrotomes rely on manual adjustments by the operator to control the cutting process. On the other hand, Computer Control ultramicrotomes are equipped with advanced software that allows for precise and automated control of cutting parameters. The Tactile Control market caters to users who prefer a hands-on approach to sample preparation, while the Computer Control market targets those seeking more precise and efficient cutting processes.</p></p>
