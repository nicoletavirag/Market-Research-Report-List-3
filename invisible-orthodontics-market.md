This technology allows orthodontists to create a 3D model of a patient's teeth, which can then be used to design custom aligners with greater precision. Lingual Braces are attached to the back of teeth, making them completely hidden from view. These options offer more discreet orthodontic treatment compared to traditional metal braces, appealing to individuals seeking a less noticeable way to straighten their teeth.</p></p>
