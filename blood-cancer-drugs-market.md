<p><p>Blood cancer drugs market can be broadly categorized into two types: Blood Chemotherapy Drugs and Blood Targeted Therapy Drugs. Blood Chemotherapy Drugs are designed to destroy cancer cells by interfering with their growth and reproduction. These drugs are administered systemically through intravenous infusion or oral medications. On the other hand, Blood Targeted Therapy Drugs work by targeting specific molecules that are necessary for cancer cell survival and proliferation. This type of therapy aims to minimize damage to healthy cells and reduce the side effects associated with traditional chemotherapy.</p></p>
