<p><p>Laboratory monocular microscopes come in two main types - Inverted Type and Upright Type. Inverted Type microscopes are designed with the objective lens under the stage, allowing for the observation of samples in dishes or tall containers. Upright Type microscopes have the objective lens above the stage, making it suitable for observing slides and small specimens placed on the stage. Both types of microscopes are widely used in laboratories for a variety of scientific research and analysis purposes.</p></p>
