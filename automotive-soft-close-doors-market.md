<p><p>Automotive soft-close doors come in two types: automatic and manual. Automatic soft-close doors use sensors to detect when a door is not fully closed and automatically pull it shut. This feature is often found in luxury vehicles and provides added convenience and safety. Manual soft-close doors require the driver or passenger to gently push the door once it is almost closed, and the mechanism will pull it shut. Both types of soft-close doors aim to prevent slamming and ensure a secure seal while also giving a premium feel to the vehicle.</p></p>
