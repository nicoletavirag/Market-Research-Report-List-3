<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Semi-automatic</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric bending tools come in three main types - manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual bending tools require physical effort from the operator to bend the material, making them suitable for smaller projects or occasional use. Semi-automatic bending tools have some automated features, such as preset angles or digital displays, to assist the operator in bending accurately and efficiently. Automatic bending tools are fully automated, able to bend materials without operator intervention, making them ideal for high-volume production and complex bending projects.</p></p>
