<p><p>Antiblock masterbatch market types can be classified into organic type and inorganic type. In contrast, inorganic type masterbatches contain additives that are synthetically produced, often using minerals or chemicals. Both types of masterbatches serve the same purpose of improving the slip properties of plastic films, preventing them from sticking together.
