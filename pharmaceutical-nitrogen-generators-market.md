<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Nitrogen Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Swing Adsorption</li><li>Membrane</li><li>Cryogenic Air</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical Nitrogen Generators use different technologies to produce nitrogen gas for pharmaceutical applications. Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) generators use adsorbent materials to separate nitrogen from air. Membrane generators use semi-permeable membranes to separate nitrogen from other gases. Cryogenic Air generators cool and compress air to extract nitrogen. Each technology has its own advantages and cost considerations. The choice of generator type depends on factors such as purity requirements, output capacity, and operating costs in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
