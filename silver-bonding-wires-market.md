<p><p>Silver bonding wires are commonly used in semiconductor packaging due to their high electrical conductivity and reliability. There are two main types in the market - SEA Type and SEB Type. SEA Type bonding wires are known for their excellent bondability and resistance to heat, making them ideal for high-temperature applications.
