<p><strong>The Nitrogen Purging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dilution Nitrogen Purging</li><li>Displacement Nitrogen Purging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitrogen purging systems are commonly used in industrial processes to remove or displace oxygen and other contaminants from tanks, pipelines, and other equipment. Dilution nitrogen purging involves mixing nitrogen with the existing gases to reduce the oxygen content to a safe level. Displacement nitrogen purging, on the other hand, involves completely replacing the existing gases with nitrogen to create an inert environment. Both methods are essential for ensuring safety and preventing hazards in various industries such as oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and electronics.</p></p>
