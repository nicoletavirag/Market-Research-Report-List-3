<p><p>Metal pallet pooling is a cost-effective and sustainable solution for businesses to utilize reusable pallets. Pallet rental involves companies renting pallets for a specific timeframe, paying only for the duration they are in use. Pallet pooling is a shared system where multiple businesses share pallets within a closed-loop system, reducing the need for constant purchasing and disposal. Both options offer flexibility and cost savings, making them popular choices in the logistics industry.</p></p>
