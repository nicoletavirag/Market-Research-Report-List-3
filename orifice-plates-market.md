<p><p>Orifice Plates are commonly categorized into three types: Universal Orifice Plates, Special Orifice Plates, and Paddle Orifice Plates. Universal Orifice Plates are the most commonly used and are suitable for a wide range of applications. Special Orifice Plates are designed for specific requirements or challenging conditions. Paddle Orifice Plates have a solid plate with a central hole, which is used for measuring flow in large pipe diameters. Each type serves a specific purpose and is chosen based on the application requirements.</p></p>
