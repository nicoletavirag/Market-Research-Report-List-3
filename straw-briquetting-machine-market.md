<p><p>Straw briquetting machines are classified into three main types: mechanical, hydraulic, and screw extrusion. Mechanical machines use a lever or crank to compress straw into briquettes. Hydraulic machines utilize hydraulic pressure to compress the straw. Screw extrusion machines utilize a screw mechanism to pressurize and compact the straw into briquettes. Each type of machine offers unique advantages and features, catering to different market preferences and needs in the straw briquetting industry.</p></p>
