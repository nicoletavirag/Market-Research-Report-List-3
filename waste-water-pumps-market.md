<p><p>Waste Water Pumps market can be broadly categorized into two types - Submersible Type and Dry-Type. Submersible Type pumps are designed to be submerged in water and are ideal for applications where there is a need to pump water from deep underground. Dry-Type pumps, on the other hand, are installed above the water level and are suitable for applications where the pump needs to be easily accessible for maintenance or repairs. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in the waste water management sector.</p></p>
