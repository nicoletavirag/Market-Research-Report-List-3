<p><p>Pet Allergy Treatment Drugs market involves several types of drugs such as Antihistamines, Decongestants, Corticosteroids, and others. Antihistamines work by blocking the action of histamines, which are chemicals released by the immune system in response to allergens. Decongestants reduce nasal congestion by constricting blood vessels in the nasal passages. Corticosteroids reduce inflammation and immune system responses. Other drugs may include leukotriene modifiers and mast cell stabilizers which work by targeting different pathways in the allergic response.</p></p>
