<p><p>The downhole casing market consists of various types, including surface casing, oil layer casing, and other markets such as intermediate casing and production casing. Surface casing is used to protect freshwater zones and support the wellbore, while oil layer casing is used to isolate and protect specific production zones. Other markets like intermediate and production casing serve different purposes in the drilling process, such as providing structural integrity, managing fluids, and preventing formation damage. Each type of casing plays a crucial role in ensuring the success and safety of oil and gas drilling operations.</p></p>
