<p><p>5G Network Infrastructure Market Types are categorized based on the size and coverage area they cater to. Femto Cells are small, low-powered base stations ideal for indoor coverage in homes or small businesses. Pico Cells have a slightly larger coverage area and are commonly used in enterprises or urban areas. Micro Cells cover larger areas like stadiums or shopping malls. Macro Cells are large base stations providing broad coverage in urban or rural areas. Each market type serves specific needs based on coverage and capacity requirements.</p></p>
