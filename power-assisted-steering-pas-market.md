<p><p>Power Assisted Steering (PAS) systems are categorized into three main types: C-EPS, P-EPS, and R-EPS. C-EPS, or Column-mounted Electric Power Steering, is mounted on the steering column. P-EPS, or Pinion-mounted Electric Power Steering, is mounted on the steering gear. R-EPS, or Rack-mounted Electric Power Steering, is mounted on the rack and pinion. Each type of PAS system offers unique benefits and features, catering to different vehicle requirements and driving preferences.</p></p>
