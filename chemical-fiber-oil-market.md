<p><strong>The Chemical Fiber Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DTY</li><li>FDY</li><li>POY</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical Fiber Oil market has three main types: DTY (Draw Textured Yarn), FDY (Fully Drawn Yarn), and POY (Partially Oriented Yarn). DTY is highly elastic and commonly used in the production of sportswear and outerwear. FDY is smooth and strong, making it ideal for use in the production of textiles and clothing. POY is known for its durability and resistance to abrasion, making it suitable for use in products like carpets and industrial fabrics. Each type of fiber oil caters to specific industries and applications.</p></p>
