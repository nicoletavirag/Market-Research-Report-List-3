<p><p>Potting soil mix market is segmented into various types based on the materials used. Soilless mixes are made without traditional soil, using ingredients like peat moss and vermiculite. Composts are organic mixes made from decomposed plant material. Gravel mixes provide good drainage for plants. Inert media mixes contain materials like sand and perlite. Other types of mixes may include coco coir or pine bark. Each type of mix offers different benefits for plant growth and health, catering to various gardening needs.</p></p>
