Ductile iron is known for its high tensile strength and impact resistance, making it ideal for parts subjected to heavy loads. Malleable iron is highly ductile and tough, making it suitable for applications that require flexibility and durability. Each type of iron casting serves a specific purpose and offers unique advantages in various industries.</p></p>
