<p><p>Infrared Thermal Imaging Camera market can be categorized into two main types: Cooled Type and Uncooled Type. On the other hand, Uncooled Type cameras do not require cooling mechanisms, making them more cost-effective and easier to maintain. Both types have their own advantages and applications, with Cooled Type being more suitable for high-end applications requiring precise temperature measurements, while Uncooled Type is preferred for general purpose use.</p></p>
