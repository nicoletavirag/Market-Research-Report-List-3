<p><p>High-voltage grounding switch market offers two main types: independent earthing switches and earthing switches with combined current transformers. Independent earthing switches provide a dedicated ground connection for high-voltage equipment, ensuring safety during maintenance. On the other hand, earthing switches with combined current transformers offer the additional function of measuring and monitoring currents flowing through the switch, providing valuable data for operational purposes. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of high-voltage electrical systems.</p></p>
