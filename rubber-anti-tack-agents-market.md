There are various types of rubber anti-tack agents available, including stearates, fatty acid esters, fatty acid amides, and soaps. Stearates are metal salts of fatty acids, fatty acid esters are derived from fatty acids and alcohols, fatty acid amides are derived from fatty acids and amines, and soaps are metallic salts of fatty acids. Each type of anti-tack agent offers unique properties and benefits for rubber processing.</p></p>
