<p><p>Personal fall protection equipment market consists of various types including safety harnesses, safety lanyards, anchor points, and self-retracting lifelines. Safety harnesses are worn around the torso to prevent falls, while safety lanyards are used to provide connection between the harness and an anchor point. Anchor points are fixed structures for securing equipment, while self-retracting lifelines automatically stop a fall. Other types of fall protection equipment include guardrails, safety nets, and rope grabs, all designed to ensure worker safety at heights.</p></p>
