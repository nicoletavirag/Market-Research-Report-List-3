<p><p>Plant growth modifiers are substances that regulate the growth and development of plants. There are different types of plant growth modifiers in the market such as auxin, cytokinins, and gibberellins. Auxins are responsible for promoting cell elongation, cytokinins stimulate cell division, and gibberellins promote stem and leaf growth. Other plant growth modifiers include ethylene, abscisic acid, and brassinosteroids. Each type of growth modifier plays a specific role in influencing different aspects of plant growth, ultimately leading to healthier and more productive crops.</p></p>
