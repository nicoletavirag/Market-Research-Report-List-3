<p><ul><li>Single-Phase</li><li>Duplex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hyaluronic acid fillers come in two main types: single-phase and duplex. Single-phase fillers are made up of uniform particles of hyaluronic acid, providing a smooth and consistent result when injected into the skin. Duplex fillers, on the other hand, consist of a mix of cross-linked and linear hyaluronic acid particles, offering both immediate volumizing effects and longer-lasting results. Both types of fillers are commonly used in cosmetic procedures to restore volume, improve skin hydration, and reduce the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines.</p></p>
