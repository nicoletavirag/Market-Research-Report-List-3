<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Oil-Well Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary</li><li>Moderate Sulfate-Resistant</li><li>High Sulfate-Resistant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil-well cement is categorized into three main types based on their resistance to sulfate attack: Ordinary, Moderate Sulfate-Resistant, and High Sulfate-Resistant. Ordinary oil-well cement is used in wells with low sulfate concentrations, while Moderate Sulfate-Resistant cement is suitable for moderate sulfate conditions. High Sulfate-Resistant cement is specifically designed for wells with high sulfate levels to prevent degradation and ensure long-term structural integrity. Each type of cement offers varying degrees of protection against sulfate attack to cater to different well environments.</p></p>
