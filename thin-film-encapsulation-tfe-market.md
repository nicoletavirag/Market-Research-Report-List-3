<p><p>Thin-Film Encapsulation (TFE) market consists of two main types: Organic Layers and Inorganic Layers. Organic Layers utilize organic materials such as polymers to provide a barrier against moisture and oxygen. These layers are flexible and ideal for applications where bending or flexibility is required. In contrast, Inorganic Layers use materials like metals or ceramics to create a rigid barrier. These layers are more durable and provide better protection against external factors. Both types offer unique advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
