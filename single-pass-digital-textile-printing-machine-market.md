The two main types of these machines are inkjet digital printing machines and dye-sublimation digital printing machines. Inkjet digital printing machines use inkjet technology to directly print designs onto fabrics, while dye-sublimation digital printing machines use heat to transfer dye onto fabrics. Both types of machines offer high-speed and efficient printing capabilities, making them popular choices for textile manufacturers looking for quick and high-quality printing solutions.</p></p>
