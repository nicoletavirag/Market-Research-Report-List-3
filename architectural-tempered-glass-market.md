<p><ul><li>Flat Tempered Glass</li><li>Bent Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Architectural tempered glass market offers two main types - flat tempered glass and bent tempered glass. Flat tempered glass is produced by heating and cooling glass uniformly to increase its strength, making it suitable for various architectural applications such as windows, doors, facades, and partitions. On the other hand, bent tempered glass is created by shaping the glass while it is still in a pliable state, resulting in curved or custom shapes, ideal for creating unique architectural designs like storefronts, skylights, and railings.</p></p>
