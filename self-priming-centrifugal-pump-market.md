<p><p>Self Priming Centrifugal Pump Market can be classified into two major types: Multistage Self-Priming Pump and Single Stage Self-Priming Pump. Multistage Self-Priming Pump is designed with multiple impellers to increase pressure, making it suitable for high-pressure applications. On the other hand, Single Stage Self-Priming Pump has only one impeller and is used for low to medium-pressure applications. Both types are efficient in handling liquids with entrained air and are widely used in various industries for their self-priming capabilities and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
