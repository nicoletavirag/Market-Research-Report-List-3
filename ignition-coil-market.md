<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Spark</li><li>Multi-Spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-spark ignition coils produce one spark per ignition cycle, making them simpler and more cost-effective. They are commonly used in older vehicles and basic engine setups. On the other hand, multi-spark ignition coils can produce multiple sparks within a single ignition cycle, resulting in more efficient combustion and better performance. These types are often found in modern vehicles with advanced engine technology and high-performance requirements. Both single-spark and multi-spark ignition coils play crucial roles in ensuring proper ignition timing and combustion in internal combustion engines.</p></p>
