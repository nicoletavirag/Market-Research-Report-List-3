<p><p>Ceilometers are atmospheric instruments used to measure cloud height and vertical visibility. There are two main market types of ceilometers: Laser-drum ceilometers and Optical ceilometers. Laser-drum ceilometers use laser beams to determine cloud heights, while Optical ceilometers use optical sensors. Both types of ceilometers offer accurate and reliable measurements for meteorological purposes. Laser-drum ceilometers are commonly used for high-altitude applications, while Optical ceilometers are preferred for lower altitudes due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation.</p></p>
