<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Brake Fluids Market includes three main types: DOT 3, DOT 4, and DOT 5. DOT 3 brake fluid is glycol-based and commonly used in most vehicles. DOT 4 brake fluid has a higher boiling point and better performance under extreme conditions. DOT 5 brake fluid is silicone-based and used in specialized applications. Each type has specific characteristics and compatibility requirements, with DOT 4 being the most versatile and widely used in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
