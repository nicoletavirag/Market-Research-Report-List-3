<p><p>Heat flow apparatus market can be divided into two main types: shell heat transfer apparatus and tube heat transfer apparatus. Shell heat transfer apparatus consists of a shell with multiple tubes inside for efficient heat transfer. On the other hand, tube heat transfer apparatus consists of tubes through which the heat transfer fluid flows for exchanging heat. These apparatus are used in various industries such as chemical, petrochemical, and power generation for processes like heating, cooling, and condensation.</p></p>
