<p><p>Stainless steel forgings market encompasses various types of products like castings, hot/cold forged parts, and sintered parts. Castings are made by pouring molten metal into a mold and are often used for complex shapes. Hot and cold forged parts are formed by applying pressure to shape metal at high or low temperatures. Sintered parts are made by compacting and heating metal powder to bond particles together. Each type of forging offers unique advantages in terms of strength, durability, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
