<p><ul><li>Active Closure Devices</li><li>Passive Closure Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Large Bore Vascular Closure System Market consists of two main types: Active Closure Devices and Passive Closure Devices. Active Closure Devices use mechanisms such as collagen plugs or nitinol clips to seal the puncture site, while Passive Closure Devices rely on manual compression methods or external vascular compression devices to achieve hemostasis. These systems play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient closure of large bore vascular access sites post procedures such as transfemoral transcatheter aortic valve replacement (TAVR) or endovascular aneurysm repair (EVAR).</p></p>
