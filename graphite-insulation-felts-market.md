<p><strong>The Graphite Insulation Felts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rayon Based</li><li>PAN Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphite insulation felts can be categorized into two main types based on the material used: Rayon Based and PAN Based. Rayon based felts are made from regenerated cellulose fibers and are known for their high thermal stability and resistance to chemical degradation. On the other hand, PAN-based felts are made from polyacrylonitrile fibers and are valued for their excellent mechanical strength and thermal resistance. Both types of graphite insulation felts offer superior insulation properties and are widely used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
