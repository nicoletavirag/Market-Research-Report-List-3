<p><p>Clutches are essential components in vehicles, with three main market types: manual, semi-automatic, and full-automatic. Manual clutches require the driver to manually engage and disengage the clutch by pressing the clutch pedal. Semi-automatic clutches combine manual and automatic features, allowing the driver to manually shift gears or let the system do it automatically. Full-automatic clutches provide a fully automated shifting experience, where the system takes care of all gear changes without the need for manual intervention. Each clutch type offers unique benefits to drivers based on their preferences and driving habits.</p></p>
