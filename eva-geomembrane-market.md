<p><p>EVA geomembrane market includes three main types: single layer geomembranes, double layer geomembranes, and three layer geomembranes. Single layer geomembranes are made of a single sheet of EVA material, providing basic protection. Double layer geomembranes consist of two layers for added strength and durability. Three layer geomembranes are the most advanced, with an extra layer for enhanced resistance to punctures and tears. Each type caters to different levels of protection and durability in various applications.</p></p>
