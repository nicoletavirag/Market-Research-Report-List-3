<p><p>The Rifle Scopes market can be categorized into various types based on their design and function. Telescopic sights, also known as scopes, use lenses to magnify targets and allow for precise aiming over long distances. Collimating sights provide a simple, non-magnifying sight picture for quick target acquisition. Reflective sights use a reflective surface to project an aiming point onto the target. Other types of rifle scopes may include night vision scopes, thermal imaging scopes, and holographic sights, each offering unique features for different shooting scenarios.</p></p>
