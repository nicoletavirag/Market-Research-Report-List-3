<p><p>A Hall effect speed sensor is a device that utilizes the Hall effect to measure the speed of a moving object. The Hall effect refers to the production of a voltage difference across an electrical conductor when a magnetic field is applied in a direction perpendicular to the current flow. In the case of a speed sensor, the Hall effect is used to detect changes in magnetic fields caused by the movement of a target object, allowing for the measurement of its speed.</p><p>The Hall effect speed sensor market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period.
