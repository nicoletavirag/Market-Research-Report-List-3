<p><p>Dielectric waveguide filters are used in various applications for controlling and manipulating electromagnetic waves. These filters can be categorized into narrowband and broadband filters based on their frequency response characteristics. Narrowband filters are designed to pass a limited range of frequencies with high selectivity, making them suitable for applications requiring precise frequency control. On the other hand, broadband filters allow a wider range of frequencies to pass through, making them suitable for applications where a broader frequency range needs to be filtered.</p></p>
