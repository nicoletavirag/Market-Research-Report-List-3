<p><p>Breast Imaging Technologies market can be categorized into Ionizing and Non-Ionizing technologies. Ionizing technologies, such as mammography and digital breast tomosynthesis, use low-dose X-rays to create detailed images of breast tissue and are widely used for breast cancer screening. On the other hand, Non-Ionizing technologies, such as ultrasound and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), utilize sound waves and magnetic fields to generate images without exposing the patient to radiation. Both types of technologies play a crucial role in early detection and diagnosis of breast cancer.</p></p>
