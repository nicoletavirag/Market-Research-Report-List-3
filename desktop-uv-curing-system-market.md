<p><p>Desktop UV curing systems come in two main types - spot-type and line-type. Spot-type systems emit concentrated UV light in a small area, making them ideal for curing small parts or components. Line-type systems emit UV light in a linear pattern, allowing for larger areas to be cured in a more efficient manner. Both types of systems are compact and used for curing adhesives, coatings, and inks on various materials in industries such as electronics, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
