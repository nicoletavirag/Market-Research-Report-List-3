<p><p>Home platform lifts are available in two main market types: vertical travel only and vertical and horizontal travel lifts. Vertical travel-only lifts are designed to move only up and down, providing accessibility between different levels in a home. Vertical and horizontal travel lifts, on the other hand, offer both vertical and horizontal movement capabilities, allowing users to move not only between floors but also across different areas within a home. Both types of lifts serve the purpose of improving mobility and independence for individuals with limited mobility.</p></p>
