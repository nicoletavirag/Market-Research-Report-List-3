<p><p>Electromagnetic viscometers are classified into two main types: laboratory viscometers and process viscometers. Laboratory viscometers are designed for testing viscosity in a controlled laboratory setting, while process viscometers are used for continuous monitoring and control of viscosity in industrial processes. Laboratory viscometers are typically more precise and offer a wider range of testing capabilities, while process viscometers are rugged and reliable for use in harsh industrial environments. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring product quality and process efficiency.</p></p>
