<p><p>Aircraft jacks are essential tools for lifting and supporting aircraft during maintenance and repair. There are two main types on the market: hydraulic jacks and pneumatic jacks. Hydraulic jacks use fluid pressure to lift the aircraft, providing stable and precise control. Pneumatic jacks, on the other hand, use compressed air to achieve the same lifting capabilities. Each type has its strengths and weaknesses, with hydraulic jacks typically being more popular due to their reliability and versatility, while pneumatic jacks are favored for their lightweight and easy maneuverability.</p></p>
