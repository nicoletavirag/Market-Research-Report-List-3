<p><p>Desktop barcode scanners come in two main types: wired and wireless. Wired scanners typically connect to a computer or POS system via a USB cable, providing a secure and stable connection. They are best suited for stationary setups where mobility is not necessary. On the other hand, wireless scanners use Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to transmit data to a nearby device without the need for cables. They offer greater flexibility and freedom of movement, making them ideal for environments where mobility is key.</p></p>
