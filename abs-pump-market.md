<p><ul><li>Electronic ABS Pump</li><li>Mechanical ABS Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ABS Pump market has two main types - Electronic ABS Pump and Mechanical ABS Pump. Electronic ABS Pump uses electronic sensors and control modules to regulate brake pressure, providing more precise and efficient braking performance. On the other hand, Mechanical ABS Pump relies on mechanical components like valves and pumps to control brake pressure. Electronic ABS Pump is more advanced and commonly used in modern vehicles for its superior performance and reliability, while Mechanical ABS Pump is found in older vehicles or in low-cost models.</p></p>
