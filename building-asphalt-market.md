<p><p>Building Asphalt market is segmented into four types based on composition and properties. Polymer-Modified Bitumen is mixed with polymers to enhance durability and flexibility. Bitumen Emulsion is a mixture of bitumen and water for easy application. Cutback Emulsion contains solvents to make the bitumen diluted for better spreadability. Oxidized Emulsion is bitumen treated with air to improve its strength and resistance to aging. Each type caters to specific construction needs, providing a diverse range of options for different projects.</p></p>
