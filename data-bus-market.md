<p><p>Data Bus Market Types include ARINC 429/629, CAN, TTP, AFDC/ARINC 664, and MIL-STD-1553. ARINC 429/629 is commonly used in aerospace and aviation applications for digital communication between avionics systems. CAN is widely used in automotive and industrial applications for high-speed communication between electronic control units. TTP is a deterministic protocol used in automotive and aerospace systems for real-time communication. AFDC/ARINC 664 is used in avionics systems for high-speed Ethernet networking. MIL-STD-1553 is a standard used in military and aerospace systems for reliable data communication.</p></p>
