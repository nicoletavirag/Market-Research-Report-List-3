<p><p>The Omega-3 market is divided into two main types: Marine Omega-3 and Algae Omega-3. Marine Omega-3 is derived from fish such as anchovies, sardines, and mackerel, while Algae Omega-3 is sourced from algae. Marine Omega-3 is the most common form found in fish oil supplements and is known for its high levels of EPA and DHA. Algae Omega-3, on the other hand, is a vegetarian alternative that is popular among those following a plant-based diet or looking to avoid potential contaminants found in fish.</p></p>
