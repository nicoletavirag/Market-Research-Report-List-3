<p><strong>The Cooling Tower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporative Cooling Tower</li><li>Dry Cooling Tower</li><li>Hybrid Cooling Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cooling towers are used in various industries to remove excess heat from buildings or processes. There are three main types of cooling towers: evaporative cooling towers, dry cooling towers, and hybrid cooling towers. </p><p>Evaporative cooling towers use water to cool the air through evaporation, making it an efficient and cost-effective solution. Dry cooling towers use air to cool the water circulating through the system, making them suitable for areas with water scarcity. Hybrid cooling towers combine features of both evaporative and dry cooling towers to provide a versatile and customizable cooling solution.</p></p>
