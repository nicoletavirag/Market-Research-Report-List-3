<p><p>Automatic blood component extractors come in two main types: Single Press and Double Press. A Single Press extractor operates by pressing the blood bags once to release the desired component, while a Double Press extractor goes a step further by pressing the bags twice to ensure maximum extraction efficiency. Both types are commonly used in blood processing facilities to separate whole blood into its various components, such as red blood cells, platelets, and plasma, for use in medical treatments.</p></p>
