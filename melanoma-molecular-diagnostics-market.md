<p><p>Melanoma molecular diagnostics market includes various types of testing methods such as Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization (FISH), DNA sequencing, and Gene Chip analysis. PCR is used to amplify DNA for detection of specific genetic mutations. FISH is a method to detect chromosomal abnormalities. DNA sequencing helps in identifying genetic mutations at the nucleotide level. Gene Chip analysis involves studying gene expression patterns. These techniques play a crucial role in diagnosing and predicting outcomes in patients with melanoma.</p></p>
