<p><p>PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) & PTFE-based gaskets are used in various industries due to their excellent chemical resistance and high temperature performance. There are three main types of PTFE-based gaskets: virgin PTFE, filled PTFE, and expanded PTFE. Virgin PTFE gaskets are made from pure PTFE material, while filled PTFE gaskets contain added fillers like glass fiber or graphite for improved properties. Expanded PTFE gaskets are made by expanding PTFE to create a highly compressible material with enhanced sealing capabilities. Each type offers different advantages depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
