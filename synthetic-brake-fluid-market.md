<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Synthetic brake fluid comes in various types classified by Department of Transportation (DOT) standards. DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1 are the most common types in the market. DOT 3 and DOT 4 are glycol-based fluids suitable for most vehicles, while DOT 5 is silicone-based and suitable for classic cars and motorcycles. DOT 5.1 is a high-performance fluid that offers improved boiling points and longevity. Each type is formulated to meet specific performance requirements and should be chosen based on vehicle specifications and driving conditions.</p></p>
