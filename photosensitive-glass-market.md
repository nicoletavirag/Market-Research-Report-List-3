<p><p>Photosensitive glass is available in two main types: Transparent Glass and Opacified Glass. Transparent glass allows light to pass through, making it suitable for applications where transparency is desired. On the other hand, Opacified Glass contains particles that block light, creating a translucent or opaque appearance. Opacified Glass is often used in privacy glass, decorative glass, and in areas where diffused light is desirable. Both types of photosensitive glass offer unique aesthetic and functional properties for various architectural and design applications.</p></p>
