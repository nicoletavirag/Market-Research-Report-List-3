<p><ul><li>Millivolt Thermostats</li><li>24 Volt Thermostats</li><li>Line Voltage Thermostats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical room thermostats come in three main types: Millivolt Thermostats, 24 Volt Thermostats, and Line Voltage Thermostats. Millivolt thermostats are used for systems that operate on low voltage, such as gas fireplaces. 24 Volt thermostats are typically found in residential HVAC systems, which are powered by a control transformer. Line Voltage thermostats are used for high voltage systems, such as baseboard heaters. Each type of thermostat is designed for specific voltage systems, providing different solutions for various heating and cooling needs.</p></p>
