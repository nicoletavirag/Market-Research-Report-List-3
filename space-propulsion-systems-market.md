<p><p>Space propulsion systems market can be divided into three main types: Solid Fuel Chemical Propulsion, Liquid Fuel Chemical Propulsion, and Cold-Gas Chemical Propulsion. Solid fuel systems use a solid chemical that burns to produce thrust, while liquid fuel systems use a liquid propellant that is ignited to create thrust. Cold-gas systems, on the other hand, use compressed gas as the propellant. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different space missions and applications.</p></p>
