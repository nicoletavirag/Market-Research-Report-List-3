IC Card meters require consumers to purchase and insert a prepaid card into the meter for electricity usage, providing a convenient and controlled payment system. Non-IC Card meters operate without the need for such cards, allowing users to pay for electricity through other means such as online or mobile payments. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and provide options for efficient electricity management.</p></p>
