<p><strong>The Automotive Radiators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Down-Flow</li><li>Cross-Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive radiator market consists of two main types: down-flow radiators and cross-flow radiators. Down-flow radiators have the tanks located on the top and bottom of the core, with coolant vertically flowing through the core. Cross-flow radiators, on the other hand, have tanks located on the sides of the core, with coolant horizontally flowing through the core. Each type offers pros and cons in terms of cooling efficiency, cost, and space requirements, catering to different preferences and needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
