<p><p>Double Side Polishers are machines used for polishing flat surfaces of materials like glass, metal, or ceramics. There are two main types of double side polishers based on their orientation: Vertical Type and Horizontal Type. </p><p>Vertical Type polishers are ideal for polishing larger and heavier materials because they utilize gravity to hold the materials in place during the polishing process. Horizontal Type polishers are better suited for smaller and lighter materials as they provide more control and precision during polishing. Both types cater to specific market needs and are used in various industries for surface finishing.</p></p>
