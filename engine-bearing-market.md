<p><strong>The Engine Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Roller Bearing</li><li>Tapered Roller Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engine bearings play a crucial role in reducing friction between moving parts in an engine. The two main types of engine bearings are ball roller bearings and tapered roller bearings. Ball roller bearings contain small spherical balls that allow for smooth rotation, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Tapered roller bearings have conical rollers that are designed to handle both radial and axial loads, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types are essential components in ensuring the efficient operation and longevity of engines.</p></p>
