<p><p>Gas circuit breakers are categorized based on the voltage levels they are designed to handle. Medium Voltage (MV) circuit breakers are designed for voltages up to 35 kV, High Voltage (HV) circuit breakers are suitable for voltages between 36 kV to 72.5 kV, Extra High Voltage (EHV) circuit breakers are designed for voltages between 72.5 kV to 420 kV, and Ultra High Voltage (UHV) circuit breakers are used for voltages above 420 kV. Each type of circuit breaker is specifically designed to handle different voltage levels efficiently.</p></p>
