<p><p>Steel seamless pipes are classified into two main types based on the manufacturing process used: hot-rolled steel seamless pipes and cold-rolled steel seamless pipes. Hot-rolled pipes are produced at high temperatures, resulting in a rough surface finish but with increased strength and ductility. On the other hand, cold-rolled pipes are manufactured at lower temperatures, yielding a smoother surface finish but with slightly reduced strength and ductility. Both types have their own unique advantages and are used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
