<p><p>Cloud backup market consists of three main types: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. Private Cloud involves storing data on dedicated servers within a company's own infrastructure, ensuring higher security and control. Hybrid Cloud combines elements of public and private cloud, offering a more flexible approach to data storage by allowing companies to choose where to store different types of data based on their needs and requirements.</p></p>
