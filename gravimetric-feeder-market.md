<p><p>A gravimetric feeder is a device used in industrial processes to accurately measure and control the flow of bulk materials such as powders, granules, and pellets. It operates by weighing the material as it is fed into the system, offering precise control over the amount of material being dispensed.</p><p>The gravimetric feeder market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.50% during the forecast period.
