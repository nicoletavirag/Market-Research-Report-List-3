<p><ul><li>Rack-and-Pinion Focusers</li><li>Crayford Friction Focusers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Telescope focusers come in two main types: Rack-and-Pinion Focusers and Crayford Friction Focusers. Rack-and-Pinion focusers use a toothed mechanism to move the eyepiece back and forth, providing precise adjustments but potentially leading to backlash. On the other hand, Crayford Friction focusers utilize a smooth roller system for smoother and more accurate focusing, but may not be as precise as Rack-and-Pinion focusers. Both types cater to different preferences of astronomers and offer unique advantages in the telescope market.</p></p>
