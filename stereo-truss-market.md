<p><p>The Stereo Truss market consists of two main types: Tetrahedron and Polyhedron trusses. Tetrahedron trusses are made up of triangular faces and are commonly used for applications requiring a lightweight and simple structure. Polyhedron trusses, on the other hand, are made up of multiple polygonal faces and offer greater stability and load-bearing capacity. Both types of trusses are widely used in various industries including events, exhibitions, construction, and entertainment for their efficiency and versatility in supporting overhead loads and creating unique structural designs.</p></p>
