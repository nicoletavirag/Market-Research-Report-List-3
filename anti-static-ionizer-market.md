<p><p>The Anti Static Ionizer market consists of Contact Type Ionizers and Induction Type Ionizers. Contact Type Ionizers work by physically touching the surface being treated, while Induction Type Ionizers emit ions from a distance using electromagnetic induction. Contact Type Ionizers are typically used for smaller surface areas or localized treatment, while Induction Type Ionizers are more suitable for larger areas or where direct contact is not feasible. Both types of ionizers help to neutralize static charges and improve overall safety and cleanliness in various industries.</p></p>
