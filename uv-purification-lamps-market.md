<p><ul><li>High Boron Glass Lamps</li><li>Quartz Glass Lamps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV purification lamps are commonly classified into two types based on the material used for the lamp envelope: High Boron Glass Lamps and Quartz Glass Lamps. High Boron Glass Lamps are known for their durability and resistance to thermal shock, making them ideal for high-power applications. On the other hand, Quartz Glass Lamps are preferred for their excellent UV transparency and ability to emit a high-intensity UV radiation. Both types of lamps are widely used in water purification systems, air sterilization, and other applications requiring effective UV disinfection.</p></p>
