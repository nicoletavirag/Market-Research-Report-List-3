<p><p>Residential MCBs (Miniature Circuit Breakers) come in four types based on the number of poles they have. 1 Pole MCBs are used for single-phase circuits. 2 Pole MCBs are suitable for split-phase or 240V circuits. 3 Pole MCBs are typically used in three-phase circuits, providing protection for each phase. 4 Pole MCBs are used in three-phase circuits plus an additional neutral wire. Each type of MCB provides different levels of protection based on the electrical configuration of the circuits they are installed in.</p></p>
