<p><p>Protective paints market includes three main types: Solvent-borne coatings, Water-borne coatings, and Powder coatings. Solvent-borne coatings are traditional paints that contain volatile organic compounds. Water-borne coatings are environmentally friendly alternatives that use water as a carrier instead of solvents. Powder coatings involve applying dry powder to a surface, which is then heated to form a protective layer. Each type of protective paint offers different benefits in terms of durability, ease of application, and environmental impact.</p></p>
