<p><p>Library Interactive Kiosks market includes two main types based on technology - RFID Technology and Bar Code Recognition Technology. RFID Technology utilizes radio frequency identification to track and manage library materials, allowing for easy check-in and check-out processes. On the other hand, Bar Code Recognition Technology uses barcodes to quickly identify and catalog library items. Both types provide efficient and convenient solutions for library patrons and staff, helping to streamline operations and enhance the overall library experience.</p></p>
