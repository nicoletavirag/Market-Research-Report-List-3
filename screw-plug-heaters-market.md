<p><p>Screw plug heaters are available with different types of temperature control systems:</p><p>1. Mechanical thermostat: These heaters are equipped with a simple mechanical thermostat that regulates the temperature by opening or closing the electrical circuit when a certain temperature is reached.</p><p>2. Thermocouple: Screw plug heaters with thermocouples use the principle of thermoelectricity to accurately measure temperature and provide precise control.</p><p>3. RTD sensor: These heaters use a Resistance Temperature Detector (RTD) sensor to monitor temperature changes and adjust the heating element accordingly for efficient and reliable temperature control.</p></p>
