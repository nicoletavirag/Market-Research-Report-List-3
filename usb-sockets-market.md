<p><p>USB sockets market can be categorized into two main types: wall-mounted USB sockets and surface-mounted USB sockets. Wall-mounted USB sockets are designed to be installed directly into the electrical wiring of a wall, providing a sleek and seamless look. On the other hand, surface-mounted USB sockets are mounted on the surface of a wall or other flat surface, making them ideal for installations where cutting into the wall is not possible or practical. Both types offer convenience and accessibility for charging electronic devices.</p></p>
