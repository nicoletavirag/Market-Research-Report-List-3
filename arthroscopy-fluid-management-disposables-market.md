<p><ul><li>Pump Management Disposables</li><li>Gravity Management Disposables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arthroscopy fluid management disposables refer to the tools and equipment used during arthroscopic procedures to manage the flow and pressure of irrigation fluids within the joint. There are two main types of disposables used for fluid management: pump management disposables and gravity management disposables. Pump management disposables involve the use of specialized pumps to control the flow of fluid, while gravity management disposables rely on gravity to regulate the irrigation fluid. Both types are essential for maintaining clear visibility and proper fluid pressure during arthroscopic surgery.</p></p>
