<p><p>PVD coating equipment market comprises of three main types: 1) Evaporation Coating Machines, which use a heated source to evaporate material and deposit a thin film on a substrate. 2) Sputtering Film Coaters, which use a plasma to eject molecules from a target material onto a substrate. 3) Other market options, including ion plating, cathodic arc deposition, and magnetron sputtering. Each type of equipment offers unique advantages and is utilized in various industries for coating applications.</p></p>
