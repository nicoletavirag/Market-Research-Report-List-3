<p><strong>The Heat Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Temperature Detectors</li><li>Differential Temperature Detectors</li><li>Poor Fixed Temperature Detectors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat detectors can be categorized into different types based on their functioning. Fixed temperature detectors operate by sensing a specific temperature threshold and activating an alarm when this temperature is reached. Differential temperature detectors detect the rate of temperature rise and trigger an alarm if the temperature increases rapidly. Poor fixed temperature detectors have a wider range of temperature sensitivity compared to fixed temperature detectors. Other market types may include rate-compensated detectors or combination detectors that offer multiple sensing technologies for enhanced fire detection capabilities.</p></p>
