<p><p>PUF panels are available in three main types based on thickness. Panels below 51 mm are commonly used for residential and light commercial construction due to their flexibility and ease of installation. Panels between 51 mm and 100 mm are ideal for medium to heavy commercial and industrial applications, providing enhanced insulation and structural strength. Panels above 100 mm are used for extreme temperature or high-load bearing applications where superior insulation and durability are required. Each type of panel caters to specific needs and requirements in construction projects.</p></p>
